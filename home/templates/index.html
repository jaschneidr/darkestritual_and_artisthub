Jason Schneider

Jason Schneider has been playing guitar and singing for as long as he can remember. Darkest Ritual came out of his love for music and creating. Now, Jason spends most of his days working as a software developer and spends his free time balancing between spending time with his wife and many pets (four cats, a greyhound, and a bearded dragon!), tending his magnificent garden of chili peppers, and of course, writing and recording new music for Darkest Ritual.
